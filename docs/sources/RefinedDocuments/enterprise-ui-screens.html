What is our current vendor SLA policy for P1 incidents, and who are the escalation contacts?
Per our vendor management policy, P1 Critical incidents require a 4-hour initial response SLA with resolution targets of 8 hours for full service restoration. 📄 Vendor Policy v3 · §3.2

Escalation goes through the Vendor Relationship Manager first, then VP of Operations if unresolved after 2 hours. Tier 1 vendors must provide a named TAM reachable 24/7. 📋 IT Compliance Guide · p.14

Critical infrastructure vendors additionally require quarterly SLA audits and documented P1 runbooks. 📄 Infrastructure Standards · §7.1
