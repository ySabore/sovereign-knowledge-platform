# venture outreach pack — law firms

## ideal targets

- firms with 10–100 attorneys
- practice areas with heavy precedent and document reuse: plaintiff litigation, employment, immigration, estate planning, family law, personal injury
- firms already paying for clio, netdocuments, sharepoint, imanage, or large shared drives
- managing partner, operations lead, innovation lead, practice group lead, or senior paralegal owner

## positioning in one sentence

we give firms a private ai knowledge assistant that answers from their own briefs, templates, motions, and internal know-how without turning the team into prompt engineers.

## 30-second opener

most small and mid-sized firms already have the knowledge they need, but it is scattered across briefs, templates, folders, emails, and the heads of a few senior people. we help firms turn that into a private searchable assistant so attorneys and staff can find the right internal material faster, reuse stronger work product, and onboard people without hunting through ten systems.

## if they ask “why now?”

- firms already have enough internal content to create value now
- associates and staff waste real time searching for prior work product
- partners want leverage without lowering quality
- firms are curious about ai but hesitant to risk confidentiality
- this gives them a contained internal use case instead of a public-tool free-for-all

## if they ask “what makes firms respond?”

- faster finding of prior briefs, motions, templates, and research
- quicker onboarding of associates, paralegals, and new practice staff
- less dependence on asking the same senior people for where things live
- private deployment options for confidentiality-sensitive environments
- answer grounding in the firm’s own documents instead of generic internet output

- grounded answers tied to source documents
- deployable for confidentiality-sensitive environments
